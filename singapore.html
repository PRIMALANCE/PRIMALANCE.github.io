COUNTRIES I WANT TO VISIT

SINGAPORE

SINGAPORE

SINGAPORE is special to me because of its incredible blend of modern innovation, cultural diversity, and world-class city planning. Despite being a small country, it stands out with its futuristic skyline, efficient public transportation, and clean, organized environment. I admire how Singapore balances urban development with nature, with places like Gardens by the Bay and Sentosa offering stunning views. The country’s mix of cultures—Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Western influences—also makes it unique, creating a rich food scene and vibrant traditions.

Getting to Singapore is convenient, with direct flights from Manila to Changi Airport taking around three to four hours. Changi is known as one of the best airports in the world, offering an amazing travel experience. Once in Singapore, the MRT system makes it easy to get around, and since the country is relatively small, traveling between attractions is quick and hassle-free.

While in Singapore, I’d love to visit Marina Bay Sands for its breathtaking skyline views, explore Sentosa for its beaches and theme parks, and walk through the iconic Jewel Changi with its indoor waterfall. The food scene is another must-experience, from hawker centers serving Hainanese chicken rice and laksa to high-end restaurants with Michelin-star dishes. I’d also love to see the Singapore Grand Prix circuit and enjoy the nightlife at Clarke Quay. With so much to offer, Singapore would be an unforgettable destination to explore.
