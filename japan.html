COUNTRIES I WANT TO VISIT

JAPAN

JAPAN

Japan is special to me because it perfectly blends tradition and modernity in a way that fascinates me. Its culture, from samurai history to tea ceremonies and Shinto shrines, feels like stepping into a different world. Anime, which I’ve enjoyed for years, originates here, and visiting places like Akihabara or Ghibli Museum would be a dream come true. The food is another reason—whether it’s a warm bowl of ramen, fresh sushi, or crispy tempura, Japanese cuisine is something I’d love to experience firsthand. And of course, the country’s advanced technology amazes me, from high-speed trains to robotics and gaming innovations.

To get there, I’d take a direct flight from Manila to Tokyo’s Narita or Haneda Airport, which usually takes around four to five hours. Once in Japan, the Shinkansen (bullet train) makes traveling between cities fast and convenient.

While in Japan, besides exploring anime hotspots and trying out the food, I’d love to visit Kyoto for its traditional temples, Osaka for its street food and entertainment districts, and Hokkaido for its breathtaking winter scenery. Tokyo itself has so much to offer, from Shibuya’s bustling streets to Akihabara’s anime paradise and Odaiba’s futuristic attractions. Experiencing Japan firsthand would be an unforgettable adventure.
