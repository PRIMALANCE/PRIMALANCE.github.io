COUNTRIES I WANT TO VISIT

FRANCE

FRANCE

FRANCE is special to me because of its rich history, breathtaking architecture, and deep cultural influence. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the artistic masterpieces in the Louvre, France has a timeless charm that fascinates me. Its romantic streets, elegant fashion scene, and deep appreciation for art, music, and literature make it a dream destination. I also admire how France blends tradition with modernity—whether in its historic châteaux, high-speed trains, or cutting-edge culinary techniques.

Getting to France would involve taking a long-haul flight from Manila to Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG), which usually takes around 16 to 18 hours, often with a layover. Once in France, the country’s excellent public transport system, including the TGV high-speed trains and metro networks, makes it easy to explore both Paris and other regions like Provence, Normandy, or the French Riviera.

While in France, I’d love to visit the Eiffel Tower and take in the views of Paris, explore the artistic streets of Montmartre, and experience the beauty of the Palace of Versailles. Trying authentic French cuisine—like croissants, escargots, and coq au vin—would be a must. I’d also love to visit the scenic countryside, explore the lavender fields of Provence, and see the beaches of Nice. Watching a football match at Parc des Princes or exploring Disneyland Paris would make the trip even more exciting. France’s mix of history, art, and modern lifestyle would make visiting an unforgettable experience.
